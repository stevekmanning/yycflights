    How it works

    1

    Set a price alert

    Choose a destination from YYC, pick your travel window and the max price you want to pay. You can set up to 5 alerts.

    2

    We search automatically

    Every 12 hours, we check Google Flights across 3 dates per month in your window — early, mid, and late month. That's up to 9 searches per alert per run.

    3

    Get an email when prices drop

    The moment a price falls below your threshold, we send you an email with the fare and a direct link to book it on Google Flights.

    4

    Track trends & get advice

    After 5+ price checks, we show you whether prices are rising or falling and give a buy/wait recommendation. Open Analysis on any alert card to see the full breakdown.

    All-in vs base fare

    All-in price includes all taxes and fees — the total you actually pay. Base fare is before taxes (~15% added on top). Set your threshold to match how you like to think about prices.

    Booking deadline

    If you add a deadline, we factor it into the advice (e.g. "only 4 days left — buy now") and automatically stop checking after that date.

    Can I book directly?

    Yes — every price in the preview and in email alerts includes a Book ↗ link that takes you straight to Google Flights with the exact search pre-filled.
